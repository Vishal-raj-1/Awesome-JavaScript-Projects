How To Play!



Make match of cards having same pattern-print. You win if you are able to open all cards present in the game.
To make your game :-
  1. Select one of the four dimension buttons i.e. 4X4, 4X6, 4X8, 6X8.
  2. Choose from 2 card game or 4 card game, 2 card implies that there are two cards in the game with same print and so open two cards at a time. Similar is the case with 4 card game also.
  3. Add design to your cards with options below "play again" button.
  4. Start your game by clicking on cards to reveal them.


Play this memory game and challenge your friends with time taken and number of moves you succeeded in.
